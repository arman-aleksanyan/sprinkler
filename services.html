Services & Repair

Sprinkler Company offers a wide package of maintenance and repairs services to let us handle your irrigation care all season long.

Irrigation System Design

Our certified designers spend the time you and discuss all your needs and requirements and create an economical solution for the sprinkler system design.

Installation

Irrigation system installations usually require less disruption to a property than people imagine. We work quickly and conscientiously, always careful to prevent damage to the surrounding areas.

Maintenance

We provide different maintenance services, from spring system start up to end of season fall visit to keep your irrigation system varry free.

System Repairs

The technicians at Sprinkler company are well equipped for all of your sprinkler system repair needs. Our technicians are highly trained and experienced. Our vehicles are fully equipped with the latest technology for valve location and ground fault location.

Winterization

Prevent your sprinkler system’s pipes from freezing up and busting during the frigid winter months by winterizing your sprinkler system before it’s too late. Schedule an appointment with one of our highly trained and experienced expert technicians.

Backflow Testing

It’s important to have your sprinkler system’s backflow preventer device annually inspected to make sure it provides solid protection against the health hazards of a contaminated water supply.Let the pros at Professional Irrigation Systems come out and inspect your irrigation system for breaks or leaks, and ensure that you have a safe and secure water supply for your sprinkler system.
